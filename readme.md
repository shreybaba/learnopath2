## 🌟 Why LearnoPath?

Choosing the right course shouldn't feel like finding a needle in a haystack.

Every day, students face **decision fatigue** from thousands of course options. Working professionals waste hours sifting through content that doesn't match their goals. Academic advisors can't personally guide every learner at scale.

**LearnoPath solves this.** It learns what *you* care about and surfaces exactly what you need — no fluff, no guesswork.

---

## ✨ Features

| Feature | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| 🧠 **AI Recommendations** | Courses curated based on your interests, history, and career destination |
| 🔀 **Course Comparison** | Compare courses side-by-side — ratings, duration, cost, and outcomes |
| 🗺️ **Learning Roadmap** | A personalised, step-by-step path from where you are to where you want to be |
| 💸 **Budget Aware** | Set your budget and weekly hours; get suggestions that fit your life |
| 🛠️ **Skill Gap Analysis** | Input existing skills; the AI identifies what you're missing |
| 🎓 **Career-First Approach** | Define your destination; LearnoPath reverse-engineers your path |
| 🔗 **Direct Course Links** | Recommended courses with clickable links — no extra searching required |
| 👤 **Guest Access** | Try it instantly without signing up |

---

## 🚀 How It Works

```
1. 🔐  Log in  →  or continue as a Guest
2. 📚  Choose your discipline
3. 💰  Set your budget & weekly time commitment
4. 🧩  Mention your existing skills
5. 🎯  Define your career destination
6. ✅  Get recommended courses with links + a personalised roadmap
7. ⚖️  Use the comparison tool to pick the best fit
```

It's that simple.
